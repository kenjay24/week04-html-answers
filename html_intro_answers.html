Solo Leveling

A generic power fantasy where an F-ranked Hunter, the weakest in the world, got a chance to become stronger by the gaining a system and have the capability to level up.

Characters

After sacrificing himself for the safety of others and almost dying from the ordeal he was granted a system that has an ability to level up giving him the chance to become stronger and break from being an F-ranked Hunter.
