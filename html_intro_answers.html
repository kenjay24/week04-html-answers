Solo Leveling

A generic power fantasy where an F-ranked Hunter, the weakest in the world, got a chance to become stronger by the gaining a system and have the capability to level up.

Characters

  • Sung Jinwoo

    An F-ranked Hunter from South Korea where his only determination in the dangerous world is because his sicked mother and his sister. After sacrificing himself for the safety of others and almost dying from the ordeal he was granted a system that has an ability to level up giving him the chance to become stronger and break from being an F-ranked Hunter.
